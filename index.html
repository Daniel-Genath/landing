HECair's mission
is to establish a network for all HEC students who are interested in working in commercial aviation, in Aviation Consulting, or have just an enormous personal interest or hobby for the industry.

We deeply believe that Aviation is a force for good. It enables...

  • personal growth

  • long-distance relationships

  • trade of essentiell goods to remote places

  • understanding among nations
